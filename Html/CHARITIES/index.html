Whether you need a complete rebuild or a revamp of an existing site, we can work with most platforms and systems. Our user centred design process starts with a thorough understanding of your audience, from those you are trying to help through to those all important supporters who want to help you. We also look at your in house systems and current processes to see if we can help streamline activities, save you time and let you get on with your charities core goals.

Our content management system of choice is Umbraco and we have a range of ready made modules that can quickly get you up an running informing, supporting, fundraising and recruiting. The system is as easy to use as Word and has a range of fantastic adding, editing and tracking tools that really help make life easy for you and your team. No matter how large or small your site needs to be Umbraco is fully scalable and extremely budget friendly.

The benefits of Umbraco

Key features

  • Full content management - stunningly simple editing and publishing
  • Add and edit pages, text, images and videos
  • Edit directly from Word if required
  • Site and page templates for easy creation
  • Built in media library manages all your documents, images and videos
  • Site search and site map facility so content is always accessible
  • Import content from current website
  • User access levels so multiple people can edit specific areas
  • User roles from full edit and publish to edit only and request publish approval
  • Audit trails and change logs
  • Fully responsive and mobile friendly
  • Rollback features, see previous versions and rollback as needed
  • Schedule content to go live when you want it to
  • Integration with Just Giving, PayPal, SagePay and most payment providers
  • Compatible with Google analytics to give simple yet powerful site statistics
  • Sharing with all the major social channels
  • Search engine friendly and a host of SEO tools
  • Supports multiple sites
  • Multi lingual features
  • Simple to learn and use
