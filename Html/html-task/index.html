Why choose Legend Homes?

In Tulsa Area

Executive Homes is the Tulsa home builder that makes custom, high-end finishes standard in every home we build. Whether you’re looking for a quick, move-in ready home in Midtown Tulsa, a custom new home built just for you in Broken Arrow, Bixby, or Jenks, or available lots to build on in Owasso, Executive Homes has the perfect solution to fit your needs.

What other new home builders in Tulsa consider as “add-ons,” each with an additional fee, we consider standard amenities in our new homes. Custom floor plans along with top-of-the-line finishes like granite, hardwood floors, travertine tiles, stainless steel appliances, and custom trim and cabinetry are simply basic features in each of our new homes. Thus, premium finishes normally found only in more expensive homes are standard in all Executive Homes. In addition to custom aesthetic finishes, every new home we build in the Tulsa area comes with green energy specifications that can significantly reduce your utility costs. Call us at 918.951.7009 or book a tour to see one of our furnished model homes and find out about the endless design/build possibilities for your new dream home.
