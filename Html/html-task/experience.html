Beautiful new homes are being built in Tulsa, Oklahoma; they are being offered by Legend Homes LLC, the premiere new home builders in Tulsa. We construct quality
new homes with original floor plans that are built to owner specifications. Our unique new homes in Tulsa and the surrounding area offer what many new home builders
can't, exceptional quality at an affordable price.

We currently have several gorgeous homes available in the Tulsa area. All of our homes have the quality, look, and feel of a custom home with all of the attention to
detail and higher end upgrades you would expect from quality home builders. Legend Homes is currently constructing new homes in Quail Creek. These new homes are
in Bixby with Bixby schools, close to shopping, restaurants, parks and schools.

Legend Homes is the Tulsa home builder to see first if you want a home built on your lot. We will work closely with you and all of our contractors from the beginning of
the process until we hand you the key. We have your needs in mind from start to finish. The result will be a beautiful home with exquisite attention to detail. That is why
we were awarded 1st place in the Greater Tulsa Parade of Homes in 2009!
